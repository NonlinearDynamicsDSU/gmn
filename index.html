Generative Manifold Networks (GMN)

Generative Manifold Networks are a generalization of nonlinear dynamical systems from a single manifold representing a state-space to an interconnected network of manifolds. GMN was introduced by Pao et al, and is under development at the Biological Nonlinear Dynamics Data Science Unit, OIST.
